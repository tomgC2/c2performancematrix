Fearless Innovation & Change Orientation We love improving things - processes, tools, ideas. We bring fresh thinking, curiosity, and energy to everything we do. Offers practical ideas to improve coordination tools and processes Experiments proactively with new tools and approaches Consistently drives impactful innovations to improve hiring processes Initiates and effectively spearheads holistic innovation to change initiatives Sponsors change and innovation across the C2 Talent ecosystem Champions future-oriented talent thinking at C-suite level
Experience Orientation and Ownership We care deeply about every candidate and hiring team interaction. We bring energy, empathy, and consistency to every step of the journey. Problem Solving We get things done by moving past roadblocks. We take ownership of problems, find solutions, and push hard to deliver.
